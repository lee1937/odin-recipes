Yes indeed Lasagna

A long time ago I had lasagna. Well I have had lasagna a lot. And not that long ago either because my family always has it for Christmas. Bit

of a weird tradition that one. Either way I certainly did not steal this recipe from AllRecipes. Or something like that. Who cares

  1. Combine your meats, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat. Not a pair of sheets you fart between, an actual one you goof.
  2. Once that stuff is browned stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste (YES THIS IS NECESSARY), and water
  3. Season with sugar (yes really), basil, fennel seeds, italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley
  4. Simmer that sucker for about 1.5 hours. Give it a stir and a whiff every now and then
  5. salt some water and boil it. Cook your lasagna noodles (yes they are called that)
  6. Ok I think this exercise has gone on long enough. Drain it, mix w/ ricotta, egg, and parsley
  7. spread everything out on a baking dish. layer it. bake @ 375 for 25 w/ foil, then 25 w/out.
